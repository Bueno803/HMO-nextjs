Programs & Services

Through our diverse range of programs, we address the unique needs of the people most likely to be missed. Each initiative is designed to equip participants with the tools, support, and confidence to take control of their futures while knowing they never have to navigate life alone.

Youth Development & Academic Success

Supporting system-impacted youth through daily structured programming, academic support, mentorship, SEL, and life-skills development in a culturally relevant environment.

Services Include:

  • •Big Dogg Academy
  • •Diploma Pipeline
  • •SEL & Life Lessons

Leadership, Sports & Unification

Building confidence, discipline, accountability, and community through athletics, mentorship, fitness, and positive peer connection.

Services Include:

  • •Big Dogg Sports
  • •Team-building
  • •Mentorship experiences

Workforce & Career Readiness

Preparing youth and reentry adults for sustainable futures through job-readiness training, soft skills, and professional development.

Services Include:

  • •Workforce Opportunities
  • •Stipend-based initiatives
  • •Paid internships

Healing Arts & Enrichment

Using creative expression, gardening, and transformative experiences to support healing, emotional wellness, and expanded possibilities.

Services Include:

  • •Arts & Gardening
  • •Music & Visual Arts
  • •Field Trips, College Visits, etc.

Advocacy, Diversion & Reentry Support

Providing court advocacy, violence intervention, mediation, and reentry support to help individuals navigate systems and move toward stability.

Services Include:

  • •Court Advocacy & Appointed Gang Expert Services
  • •Mitigation & Mediation
  • •Reentry Guidance

Family & Community Partnerships

Utilizing a central location, known as Upendo House, to strengthen families and communities through holistic support, collaborative partnerships, and resource navigation.

Services Include:

  • •Family Support
  • •Housing & Benefits Navigation
  • •Strategic partnership with community organizations
How We Fund Our Work

Grant-Based Approach

As a primarily grant-funded organization, we work with foundations, government agencies, and corporate partners to secure resources for our programs. This approach allows us to maintain high-quality services while ensuring accountability and transparency in how funds are used.
